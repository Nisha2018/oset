About Us

Who We Are

WE ARE OSET

The Omani Society for Education Technology (OSET) is the only association in the Sultanate of Oman that brings together educators from all government and private sectors under one umbrella. The Society provides its services to K12, Higher, Vocational and Technical Education organizations and its zone of activities cover the entire Sultanate. OSET aims to enable effective implementation and integration of technology in education. The Society was established in 1997 and was officially recognized in 2007 by the Ministry of Social Development.

  • Our Vision

Our vision is to enable educators in Oman to utilize technology to bring innovation in teaching and learning and motivate learners to reach their greatest potential.

What we do?

Enabling the Education System in Sultanate Of OMAN

  • The Omani Society for Education Technology organizes and hosts many scientific events such as seminars, symposium and conferences aimed at encouraging the integration, implementation and utilisation of emerging technology in teaching and learning. The International Conference on Educational Technology is one of the most important events that is organized by the Society and is considered one of the leading conferences in education technology in the Middle East. OSET also participates with its members in many local and international conferences and exhibitions.
  • The Society cooperates with different educational institutions and government agencies to provide consultancies and professional development on instructional applications of ICT, emerging technologies and the 4th Industrial Revolution.
  • Conduct research and studies in education technology.
  • The Society sponsors OSET members to participate in international educational conferences, exhibitions and forums.

Our Goals

What We Doing

Enable Institutions

To enable educational institutions in Oman to effectively implement and integrate technology in learning environments.

Advocating ICT

To act as an advocate on behalf of educational entities in Oman on projects related to ICT in education.

Sharing Best Practices

To help teachers and educators to establish a community of practice to share expertise and best practices, suggest interventions for tough problems and collaborate to design educational initiatives in Oman.

Digital Transformation

To facilitate the Digital Transformation in the Education.

Strategic Partnership

To encourage strategic partnerships with local and international organizations to promote excellence in learning and teaching through the innovative and effective implementation of emerging technologies.

Technology in Education

To recognise best practices in education technology and educate the community about the vital role of education technology professionals.

Digital Literacy

To foster the development of the digital literacy skills of students, educators and educational leaderships.
